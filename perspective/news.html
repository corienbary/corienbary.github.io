  • July 2021: Corien and Emar's paper on the landscape of speech reporting has been published in Semantics and Pragmatics.
  • June 2021: Kees has successfully defended his dissertation entitled 'Polysemous particles in Ancient Greek. A study with special reference to μήν and δή' (cum laude). He has accepted the invitation to publish the dissertation with Brill.
  • June 2020: The first papers of our special collection on narrative perspective entitled The Language of Perspective have been published in the Open Library of Humanities Journal. More papers will follow during the next months.
  • December 18, 2019: Leopold has successfully defended his dissertation entitled 'Perspective and Word Choice. A study of Expressives, Slurs and Narrative'. The dissertation is a collection of papers of which six have already been published or will appear soon. Check his Academia page. We wish him all the best in his new project on idealogical speech, supported by a personal grant from the Polish National Science Centre.

  • January 23, 2019: We are organizing the workshop 'Multiple Perspectives' to bring together different strands of research on narrative perspective.
