Workshop: Measuring Commitments

  • 18-19th June 2026<\li>  
  • Radboud University Nijmegen<\li>   As part of the NWO project Commitments in Communication we will organize a two-day workshop on the topic of commitment in communication, with a particular focus on experimental approaches to its study. While commitment attribution, negotiation, and avoidance play a central role in communication, its nuances remain difficult to operationalize and measure. The workshop aims to explore how experimental methods can be used to contribute to theoretical accounts. Confirmed keynote speakers: Ben Wiessman Mailin Antomo Tatjana Scheffler Manfred Krifka Organizers: Harriet Yates Bob van Tiel Thomas van der Leer Peter de Swart Corien Bary More information will follow later.
